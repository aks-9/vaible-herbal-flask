Who We Are

Premium Herbal Ingredients
Manufacturer & Exporter

Vaible Herbal is a globally focused supplier of herbal extracts, essential oils, carrier oils and botanical ingredients — manufactured in India and delivered worldwide. We combine quality-driven sourcing with modern processing to meet international B2B requirements.

Our product range supports nutraceutical, cosmetic, food and wellness formulations. With strong documentation support (COA / MSDS on request) and consistent batch-to-batch standards, we help brands and manufacturers scale with confidence.
