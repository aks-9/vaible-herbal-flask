Who We Are

A Supplier Built on Process,
Not Just Promise

Vaible Herbal is a B2B manufacturer and exporter of standardised herbal extracts, essential oils, carrier oils, herbal powders and botanical concentrates. We operate from manufacturing facilities in Himachal Pradesh, India, supplying pharmaceutical, nutraceutical and cosmetic companies worldwide.

Our foundation is not marketing. It is a sourcing discipline that begins at the botanical source — selecting the right species, part, season and geography — and continues through every stage of processing, quality control and documentation.

We do not chase volume. We build repeat relationships with buyers who value consistency, compliance, and a supplier who speaks the same quality language.
