Why This Matters

Supplying an Ingredient Is Not Enough

A herbal extract on a COA is a starting point, not a guarantee of formulation success. How a dry extract disperses in a capsule matrix, how a liquid extract behaves in a cosmetic emulsion, how an essential oil interacts with carrier systems — these are questions our B2B buyers need answers to before they commit to a formulation.

At Vaible Herbal, we document and share what we know about the application behaviour of the ingredients we supply — so formulation teams can make faster, better-informed decisions about ingredient selection and use levels.
