Marker Compounds

What Is in the Plant — and Why It Matters

Herbal extracts are not single molecules. They are complex matrices of primary and secondary metabolites — alkaloids, flavonoids, polyphenols, terpenoids, essential oils, saponins, and more. The marker compounds used for standardisation are chosen because they are measurable, stable, and associated with biological activity.

At Vaible Herbal, we understand which markers are relevant per botanical, how they behave during extraction and drying, and how natural variability — from soil, season, and geography — affects their concentration in the finished ingredient.

This understanding is what allows us to maintain specification consistency even when raw material inputs change — by adjusting process parameters rather than simply blending to hit a number.
