Advanced Non-Thermal
Decontamination Technology

Advanced non-thermal decontamination technologies are utilised at Vaible Herbal to ensure microbiological safety while preserving the natural and functional integrity of herbal ingredients. These controlled processes minimise thermal stress, helping protect heat-sensitive phytochemicals, volatile constituents, and key marker compounds.

By reducing microbial load without affecting potency, colour, aroma, or solubility, non-thermal decontamination supports consistent product quality, stability, and compliance with global pharmacopeial and quality requirements.

{# ================================================================ STERILE FILTRATION CASCADE ================================================================ #}

Quality Control

Multi-Stage Sterile Filtration
(50 µm → 0.22 µm)

Liquid herbal extracts at Vaible Herbal are processed through a validated multi-stage sterile filtration cascade designed to ensure particulate control, bioburden reduction, and microbiological safety in line with global quality expectations.

The filtration train progresses sequentially through graded cartridges: 50 µm → 10 µm → 5 µm → 1 µm → 0.22 µm. Each stage protects the next by reducing load in a controlled manner, safeguarding filter integrity and preserving the extract's functional characteristics.
